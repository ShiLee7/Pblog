Friendship

Friendship, do I know you?
I think I met you in the days of old,
But was it really you?
Are you even real on this planet blue?


My soul longs for a friend.
Many times I called people by that name,
Only to find the ship of friends had sailed.


A ghost ship is what you are—
An illusion of the innocent,
Only present on shores afar.


Now, let it be—
That phrase is my new philosophy.
I don't care about you...
You don't care about me...
