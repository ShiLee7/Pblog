Music, God's Muse

Music, God's Muse.
Was it true
that mastered by the devil
were you?


Vocal cords reproducing all
twelve tones,
wonders that no human
could ever hear—
is the power of the master of fear.


If you can stay still
while you hear the beat,
the only reason for that
is because you're bedridden, ill.


Music is a tool... remember...
it could be used to fool.
Real powers sounding at megahertz,
filling with blood your pump called heart.


Gripping thy power—
electric whisper of hell.
Hypnotize me not,
oh please, I don't want to die today.


Nietzsche was right,
he told no lie.
Without God's muse,
that's the best excuse... to stop.
