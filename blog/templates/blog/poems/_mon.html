Money, I’m a Fool

Money, I’m a fool.
Without health I cannot live;
Without you there are no green fields.


Health and true love, and maybe some other stuff,
Are the things that are more important than fancy, stupid clothes.


But money, I’m no fool.
My brain is not that of an ape.
With those things—and you—
Happiness I can obtain.


Money, I’m no fool.
My head is walking on a road;
If your green help would go along,
Everything I’ll take, everything—I’ll feel so strong.


Money, I’m no fool.
I have so much respect for you.
You turn a mother against her son;
Nations you can overthrow.


Oh God, I’m a fool.
This anxious man you have to heal.
Provide green papers, Lord,
And a good deed will be fulfilled.
